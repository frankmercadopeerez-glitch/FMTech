Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our process.

What do I need to provide to start my website?

You need to provide your logo (if you have one), the text content for the pages, and any specific images you want to use. If you don't have images, we can use high-quality stock photos to match your brand.

Is the domain and hosting really free?

Yes, all our plans include professional web hosting and one domain name (like .com or .net) free for the first year. After the first year, standard renewal fees apply, which we will inform you about in advance.

What does 'E-commerce Ready' mean?

The Premium plan includes setting up a product showcase, integrating a shopping cart, and connecting a payment gateway like PayPal. This allows you to start selling products or services directly from your site.

What if I need changes after the site is live?

For minor changes, we're here to help. For frequent updates, we highly recommend our monthly maintenance add-on, which gives you up to 4 hours of professional support for any modifications you need.

How does the payment process work?

We accept payments in USD via PayPal directly on our site for your convenience. For payments in COP, you can select your plan and click "Comenzar" (when in Spanish) to contact us and arrange the payment through other methods like bank transfer.
